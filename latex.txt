### Design Choices Explanation:

- **Main Title**: Used bold and large font size with a red color to immediately catch the audience's attention, reflecting the energy and passion of the club.
- **Mission Subtitle**: Italicized text under the main title quickly conveys the essence of the club in an elegant, inviting tone.
- **Poster Body**: A concise description in a medium font size to maintain readability, providing insight into the club's goals and welcoming tone.
- **Features Section**: Includes a cooking icon and a red color theme for the bullet points to highlight the clubâ€™s unique offerings and correspond to the passion of culinary arts.
- **Event Details**: Important information like the event name, date, and location are prominently placed for visibility, using a larger font and red highlights.
- **Contact Information**: Positioned at the bottom, using a readable size and style, ensuring interested parties can easily reach out.
- **Visual Elements**: A banner and logo add visual appeal, helping establish brand identity.
- **Social Media and Footer**: Provides easy access to further engagement, aligning with modern promotion strategies.

This layout maintains a clear hierarchy, ensuring that each piece of information flows naturally to guide the viewer's eye through the poster.
